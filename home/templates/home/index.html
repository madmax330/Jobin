Jobin Students

WHY WE DO IT

We believe in improving application processes.
We believe that applying for a job should be simple.
We believe that your time matters.

As a student, it can get overwhelming to deal with our academic goals as well as our daily responsibilities. One should not have to spend an endless amount of time on job applications in addition to that. Answering lengthy questionnaire forms and creating dozens of accounts in the quest of finding a job can certainly be exasperating (or frustrating).

Jobin was created to address all these issues. If you share our beliefs, we encourage you to start using Jobin right now.

HOW WE DO IT

By providing a simple to use platform that efficiently improves the job application process. We provide features like:

- Apply in one click
- Track all your applications in one place
- Only send cover letters when requested to do so
- Have multiple resumes on file
- Easy access to a variety of job postings
- Filter the job postings to easily find what you are looking for
- Receive notifications when there are new job postings
- See events organized by companies
- Easily withdraw an application
- Easily modify an application

So what do we do?

We give you access to a centralized platform where students find a job easily and employers can hire great individuals conveniently.

Jobin Employers

WHY WE DO IT

In our quest to create a better platform for students, we also recognized the need to improve the recruitment side of the job application process.

We believe that the tools which are currently available for recruiters are inefficient, expensive and outdated.

We believe that recruiters play a crucial role in a company’s success and deserve a resourceful platform to facilitate their tasks.

HOW WE DO IT

By providing a simple to use platform that efficiently improves the job recruitment process. We provide features like:

- Standardized resumes
- Application management tools
- Option to download a pdf version of standardized resumes
- Filter applications based on certain criteria
- Ability to send job postings to students from different schools on one platform and a user friendly and easy system
- A niche job board, where only relevant applications will be received and the ability to re-use an expired job posting

About Us

At Jobin, our mission is to help people find jobs in a way that reflects the fast paced society that we live in. We believe in changing the future of hiring. Being university students, we were frustrated with how hard it was to apply for a job. We did not understand why it was such a painful experience. We came to the conclusion that it shouldn’t be. That’s how Jobin was born.
The founders of Jobin believe in simplicity and efficiency, this platform represents just that! We hope you enjoy using this platform just as much as we enjoyed bringing it to life for you.
