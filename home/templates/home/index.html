Jobin Students

WHY WE DO IT

We believe in improving application processes.
We believe that applying for a job should be simple.
We believe that your time matters.

As a student, it can get overwhelming to deal with our academic goals as well as our daily responsibilities. One should not have to spend an endless amount of time on job applications in addition to that. Answering lengthy questionnaire forms and creating dozens of accounts in the quest of finding a job can certainly be exasperating (or frustrating).

Jobin was created to address all these issues. If you share our beliefs, we encourage you to start using Jobin right now.

HOW WE DO IT

By providing a simple to use platform that efficiently improves the job application process. We provide features like:

- Apply in one click
- Track all your applications in one place
- Only send cover letters when requested to do so
- Have multiple resumes on file
- Easy access to a variety of job postings
- Filter the job postings to easily find what you are looking for
- Receive notifications when there are new job postings
- See events organized by companies
- Easily withdraw an application
- Easily modify an application

So what do we do?

We give you access to a centralized platform where students can go to find a job easily and rapidly and employers can hire great individuals conveniently.
