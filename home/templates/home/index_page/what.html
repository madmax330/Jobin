What do we do?

At JobIn, our mission is to help people find jobs in a way that reflects the fast paced society that we live in.

Applicants shouldn’t have to fill out multiple forms for every jobs they are applying for nor should they have to bring their resume in person. We believe that applying for a job should be as easy as clicking on a button.

Student

Fellow students! All you need is your school email address, five minutes to register and setup your profile, and the willingness to find a job. Apply to the job of your dreams in a simple click!

Organization

Register today, create a job posting and use our simple and efficient talent management tool to recruit the best candidate for your company. Guess what? It’s all free!

How Jobin Works

Jobin works by facilitating the application and recruitment process for students and recruiters, respectively. Our platform allows students to easily apply to jobs and keep track of them, and it allows recruiters to create job postings, manage them and use our efficient talent management tool to choose the best fit for their company!
