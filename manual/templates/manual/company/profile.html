  • What happens to my account while it is locked due to a change of email?

    Everything in your account remains functional, but you will not be able to access it until you verify the new email address.

  • We want to change the manager of our Jobin account, how do we proceed?

    Just change the email by clicking on the 'Edit Account' button, verify the new email and optionally change the password from there (recommended).

The 'Profile' page let's you see and edit your profile and account information.

The 'Profile' page is composed of two sections:

  • User Information

    The 'User Information' section represents your account information (email, password, date joined, etc...)
    You can change this information by clicking on the 'Edit Account' button

  • Company Information

    The 'Company Information' section represents your company information (name, address, etc...)
    You can change this information by clicking on the 'Edit Profile' button
