The 'Home' page lets you quickly access the more important parts of the platform.

The 'Home' page is composed of two main sections:

  • Active Posts

    The 'Active Post’ section lets you quickly see your active posts and general information about it. You can click on the ‘Applicants’ button on the right of each active posts to see candidates that have applied for the position.

    When new applications or a cover letter has been received, the post will turn to a specific color. On the left hand side of the screen under ‘Post Legend’, you have a description of what each color represents.

    • The 'Post Title' column shows you the title of the post for that application.
    • The ‘Openings’ column shows you how many openings there are for this position.
    • The ‘Start’ and ‘Deadline’ columns shows the planned start date for the position and the application deadline.
    • The last column provides a link for quick access to that post's applicants.
  • Upcoming Events

    The 'Upcoming Events' section lets you quickly see the events that you created.
    Clicking on the 'Detail' link of an event will bring you to its detail page. (Note: the Jobin team will send you reminders (notifications and optionally email) a few days before an upcoming event, as a reminder)
