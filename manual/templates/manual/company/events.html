The ‘Event’ page lets you create and manage different events.

Events can be anything from recruitment cocktails to academic competitions.
To create an event, click on the 'New Event' button and fill out the necessary information in the form.

  • Event Details

    The 'Event Details' page shows you all the details of your event.

  • Edit Event

    The 'Edit Event' page lets you edit your event's details. (Note: any changes are updated for the viewers automatically)

Under the ‘Expired Events’ section, you have all your past events. Should you want to re-launch a past event, use the 'Recover' link to recreate your event for a later date.
