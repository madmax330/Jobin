  • What happens when I save an event?

    Saving an event will add it to your 'Saved Events' so that Jobin tracks this event for you and makes sure to remind you when it is coming up.

  • How do I save an event I’m interested in?

    To save an event, just click on the ‘Save’ button; it will then appear on your home page under ‘Saved Events’.

The ‘Events’ page lets you access the different events around you.

Those events can be anything from recruitment cocktails to academic competitions.
On each event post, you have two sections. You can navigate between the different events by clicking on the arrows to the left and right of the event box.

Each event post is composed of two sections:

  • The 'Company' section
    In this section you will find general information about the host company.
  • The 'Event Information' section
    In this section, you will find information about the event and you will be able to save the event. If you click on the green ‘Save’ button, it will appear on your home page.
