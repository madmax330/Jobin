{% endblock %} {% block faq %}
  • How do I respond to a cover letter request?

    Click on the 'Post Title' of an application with the ‘Cover letter requested’ notification to view the cover letter form and submit it.

  • How do I change the resume for an application?

    Hover of the 'Resume' of an application and select the resume you wish to use for that application.

  • Why can't I see the application I am looking for?

    The 'Active Applications' sections only displays ten applications at a time, just move on to the next page to find the application you are looking for.

  • Why can't I see the event I am looking for?

    The 'Interested Events' sections only displays ten events at a time, just move on to the next page to find the event you are looking for.

  • How will I know when a 'Saved Event' is coming up?

    Jobin will make sure to send you notifications a few days ahead to make sure you remember. (There is also an optional email notification)

{% endblock %} {% block manual %}
The 'Home' page lets you quickly access the more important parts of the platform.
  • Active Applications

    The 'Active Applications' section lets you quickly see your active applications and determine their status. When a cover letter was requested for an application, you will see a ‘Cover letter requested’ notification next to the post in the table to help you identify it faster.

    • The 'Post Title' column shows you the title of the post for that application while providing a link to the details of that post.
    • The 'Resume' column shows you the resume on file for that application; to change that resume, hover over the name of the resume and select a new on from the dropdown that appears.
    • The 'Status' column shows whether or not your application is still being considered.
    • The last column provides a link for you to withdraw your application for any reason you may have.
  • Saved Events

    The 'Saved Events' section lets you quickly see the events that you saved.
    Clicking on the 'Title' of an event will bring you to its detail page.
    The Jobin Team will send you reminders (notifications and optionally email) a few days before an upcoming event.
