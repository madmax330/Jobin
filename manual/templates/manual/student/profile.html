  • What happens to my active applications if I change my personal information?

    Any changes are updated automatically on all your active applications.

  • What happens to my active applications if I change my email?

    Your new email will appear on your active applications (which will remain active), but you will not be able to log in to your account until you verify the new email address.

The ‘Profile’ page let’s you see and edit your personal and account information.

There are three sections in the profile page:

  • The 'User Information' section
    This section contains information regarding your account such as your email address, password, date joined, etc...
    To edit this information, click on the 'Edit Account' button at the bottom of the page.
  • The 'Student Information' section
    This section contains information regarding your user profile such as your name, school, program, etc...
    To edit this information, click on the 'Edit Profile' button at the bottom of the page.
  • The 'Verify Your School' section
    This section contains information regarding your current school if you are a student, or your alma mater if you are a new graduate.
