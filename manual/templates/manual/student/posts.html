The ‘Posts’ page lets you see and apply for the job posts that we have on Jobin.
  • Types of Job Posts

    We have 5 different types of Job Postings and next to each of them, the number that is inside the parentheses represents the number posts in that category.

    • Volunteer: In this section, you will find our volunteering opportunities.
    • Internships: In this section, you will find our internship opportunities.
    • Part-time Jobs: In this section, you will find posts for jobs like bartender, barista, cashier, etc. Those are part-time student jobs.
    • Start-up: In this section, you will find job offers in start-up companies.
    • New Grad: In this section, you will find job posts pertaining to new graduates.
  • Post Details

    You can navigate between the different job posts by clicking on the arrows to the left and right of the post's box.
    Moreover, each job post is composed of three sections:

    • The 'Company' section
      Here you will find general information about the company that owns the post.
    • The 'Resumes' section
      In this section, you can see which resume the active resume. If you have more than one resume on file, you can choose which one you want to activate by clicking on it before applying to the post of your choice. (Note: it should become highlighted)
    • The 'Post Information' section
      In this section, you will find information about the post, and you will be able to apply by clicking on the 'Apply' button.
