School information

School object represent the schools that you attended during your academic journey. We recommend that you list schools starting from high school forward, but you could list your complete list of schools attended if you think it might be an asset for you.
Steps to creating a new school object:

  • Click create new school
  • Enter the name of the school
  • Enter the date you started at the school
  • Enter the date you ended at the school
  • Enter the program you were in
  • Enter the level of the institution
  • Save your new school
